Kong Studio - Essex


Built in 1749 and erected on top of an old disused cemetery this beautiful building is within walking distance of one of the finest landfill sites in the country. The property boasts 30 rooms not including it's own bunker and adjoining hellhole and offers stunning views of eternal damnation.

The current owners have refurbished this piece of architectural genius to a very high standard and are looking for a quick sale. Early viewings are highly recommended.
